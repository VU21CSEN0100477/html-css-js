The Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM) is a private deemed university located in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru³. It was founded by the late Dr. M. V. V. S. Murthi in Visakhapatnam in 1980³. GITAM offers diverse bachelor's, master's, and PhD programs in various fields such as Management, Law, Medicine, Pharmacy, Engineering, Architecture, Science, Paramedical, Physiotherapy, Nursing, Humanities & Social Sciences, and Public Policy¹. The university is known for its comprehensive education of global standards¹. It also houses the GITAM Institute of Medical Sciences and Research, a medical college and hospital in Visakhapatnam, which is home to more than 250 specialized doctors in 12 fields, and around 750 medical and paramedical students¹. GITAM has been approved by UGC and has been granted the status of a deemed university in 2007². It has three picturesque campuses at Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru⁴.
